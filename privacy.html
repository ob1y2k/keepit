  • Device information (device type, operating system, language).

  • Log and diagnostic data (crash reports, performance metrics).

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from platform providers (such as Steam, Apple, Google, or console platforms), analytics services, or payment processors, subject to their privacy policies.

We use collected information to:

We do not sell personal data. We may share information in the following situations:
